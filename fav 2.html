struggle for pleasure

  • february 2024, pixels & rotations & mirroring (medium)... an algorithmically generated image made from pixel-based symmetry, rotation, and subdivision - one of 128 unique visual outputs.
  • interplay between pixels, perception, and emotion; Embracing uncertainty, the artist invites viewers to partake in the struggle for pleasure, where simplicity unfolds into nuanced complexity.
  • “struggle for pleasure is based on the artists’ continuous engagement with foundational algorithms in computer science which began around 2005, in this case with particular focus on the implementation of a “quadtree”. Its chronological outputs are curated and manually processed into artworks that explore the interplay between pixels, perception, and emotion.
